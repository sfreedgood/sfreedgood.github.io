Early Years:

Growing up in Connecticut, Sam was heavily involved in Boy Scouts, achieving the rank of Eagle Scout. He was also an avid hockey player, until he hung up his skates in high school to pursue competitive rowing. As a rower he competed at the national level and was recruited to row Division 1 in college

College Years:

Sam went to Gonzaga University in Spokane, Washington. After rowing for a year D1, he decided to return to hockey, playing for the club team for three years. He also spent 3 summers studying chimpanzees in Zambia, as part of his psychology degree and completed a concentration in leadership studies.

Recent Years:

Since graduating college, Sam worked in the Outdoor Retail industry for 3 years in the US, New Zealand and Australia. During that time he lived a year and a half in New Zealand and another nine months in Australia. Upon leaving Australia he set about making plans to continue travelling as an English Teacher, landing in Buenos Aires, Argentina. After spending 2 years in Argentina he returned to the US and has since begun learning software development.
